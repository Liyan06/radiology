Utilizing a 1.5 Tesla unit MRI of the brain was performed and included the following sequences: sagittal T1-weighted axial fast spin echo T2-weighted axial diffusion weighted and enhanced FLAIR axial and coronal T1-weighted images. 13 milliliters of Multihance was injected intravenously.
